Pokedex Project

Name

Kanto Pokedex

Purpose

This project is a Pokedex, a digital encyclopedia of Pokémon. It allows users to browse and search for Pokémon, view detailed information about each Pokémon, and learn more about their abilities, types, and stats.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1

    Visitor: A Pokémon enthusiast who wants to learn more about their favorite Pokémon.

    Question: "I want to know more about Pikachu. What are its abilities, type, and stats?"

    Content: The website should provide a detailed page for each Pokémon, including information about their abilities, type, and stats. Users should be able to search for Pikachu and view all relevant details.

  • Scenario 2

    Visitor: A new Pokémon fan who wants to join a community of fellow enthusiasts.

    Question: "How can I join the Pokémon Club and connect with other fans?"

    Content: The website should have a dedicated page with a form for users to join the Pokémon Club. The form should collect necessary information such as name, email, and favorite Pokémon. Upon submission, users should receive a confirmation and details about the club.
